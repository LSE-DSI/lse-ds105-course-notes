Lecture (45-50 min)

This week our aim will be to understand how the internet works and begin to grasp how can we use these knowledge to mine data from the web. The first video goes through a brief explanation of how the internet works. In our live lecture we will see how to understand HTML and how can we use it to scrape data from the web. By the end of the week you should have knowledge of how the internet works, basic instruction of how to obtain data from the web, and the structure of data in the web.

Coursework

📒 Group Formation

Following the class, groups have to submit their Team Contract.

This is your first assignment of this module, worth 20% of your final marks. Your grade will be either Fail/Pass, based on whether your team contract has considered the following criteria:

  • GOALS: Has the group listed what are their current team goals are? The group could list datasets, software packages or interests (It is okay if this changes over the coming weeks)
  • EXPECTATIONS: What do we expect of one another in regard to attendance at meetings, participation, frequency of communication, the quality of work, etc.?
  • PROCEDURES: Has the group decided on the rules they would use to meet your goals and expectations?
  • CONSEQUENCES: Has the group listed actions they would take in case a colleague is not cooperative?
